Common Inquiries

How far in advance should I book? expand_more
For large events over 50 people, we recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance. For smaller gatherings, 1-2 weeks is usually sufficient, though weekends fill up quickly.
Do you provide on-site chefs and servers? expand_more
Yes, our Premium Wagyu and Signature packages include on-site carving stations and professional service staff. We bring the smokehouse atmosphere to your venue.
Can you accommodate severe nut or gluten allergies? expand_more
Absolutely. We take dietary restrictions very seriously. Our smoking process is naturally gluten-free, and we have dedicated preparation zones for allergy-safe menu items.
